Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about Lupin Spin and the LP economy.

About the Game

Is Lupin Spin a gambling game?
No. Lupin Spin is a Slot RPG that uses slots as its core loop. There's no real-money betting — the point is in-game progression and growth (treasure card upgrades, raids, LP accumulation).
Where do I play Lupin Spin?
Inside a Telegram Mini App. Open @LupinSpinDevBot in Telegram and tap the Play button to start instantly. No separate app install needed.
Is it free to play?
Yes, base gameplay is free. You just need a Telegram account.
How does energy recharge?
Energy recharges automatically over time. You can also get extra energy by inviting friends or through seasonal events.
How do I get treasure cards?
Buy and upgrade stage-specific treasure cards with the gold you earn from spins. Active cards automatically produce tokens every hour. Activating all 3 cards in the same stage triggers a synergy bonus.
Who is NOA, the manager?
A cat proxying for the god of luck, responsible for managing your treasure cards. A perpetually bothered tsundere — but the key helper who refines the treasures' aura of wealth into tokens for you.

LP & Ecosystem

What is LP?
LP (Lupin Points) is a composite asset, not a simple point: ① in-game currency, ② cross-game asset, ③ exchangeable value, ④ a way to share DreamDo's growth with you. Every play (spin · raid · card) accumulates LP.
When can LP be exchanged for tickets and rewards?
At a specific point on the roadmap, via Lupin Hub. The exact timing will be announced later.
Can I use LP in other DreamDo games?
Yes, after Lupin Hub launches. A single LP balance will be usable in DreamDo games launching later — Lupin Casino, Lupin Quest, and more.
What is DreamDo?
DreamDo is a publisher of games built as Telegram mini-apps. "Grow together. Play together." — we build experiences where players take part in a game's growth and share in it. Lupin Spin is the first title of our first series (Lupin Series).
When is Lupin Hub launching?
It's the NEXT step after Lupin Spin Beta. The exact timing will be announced later; updates go out through our Telegram channel.

Updates and announcements go out through our Telegram channel. Major updates are also reflected on this docs site.
