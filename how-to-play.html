How to Play

Spend energy to spin the slot, grow cards, and compete with other players — the basic flow of the Slot RPG.

1

Start Lupin Spin in Telegram

In Telegram, search for @LupinSpinDevBot or tap the link. Pressing the Play button below the chat opens the Mini App. No separate installation — the game starts right away.

2

First spin — burn energy, spin the slot

You're given energy when you start. Spend 1 energy for one spin. When the reels stop, the outcome moves gold, symbols, cards, and quest progress all at once.

3

Collect gold and symbols

Gold from spins is automatically added to your wallet. Collecting specific symbols completes a card quest, which rewards bonus gold.

4

Buy and upgrade treasure cards

Use your gold to buy stage-specific treasure cards. Active cards automatically produce tokens every hour. Upgrading increases the yield.

This is the step where NOA refines the treasures' aura into tokens for you.

5

Compete — Raid · Attack · Defend

Slot outcomes can give you chances to attack another player or join a raid. Your treasures can also be attacked, so defense matters too.

6

Check your LP

Every play converts into LP (Lupin Points) and accumulates. Check your total LP on your in-game profile.

LP will later be usable in other DreamDo games via Lupin Hub, and exchangeable for tickets and rewards.

🎩 Tip

  • • Energy recharges automatically over time. Drop in often and keep spinning.
  • • Activating all 3 treasure cards within the same stage triggers a synergy bonus.
  • • Invite friends for bonus energy and LP.
