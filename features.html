Features

Lupin Spin is a Slot RPG on Telegram. Slots are the core loop engine — on top sit treasure cards, raids, and the LP economy.

Core Loop

Spend energy on spins to collect gold and symbols, grow treasure cards, earn LP — that's the basic flow.

Energy → Spin → Win (Gold / Symbol / Card / Quest)
    → Raid (attack · defend · raid)
    → Gold → Treasure Card (buy · upgrade)
    → LP (Lupin Points)
    → [Future] Use in other games via Hub · Exchange for rewards
01

Slot — Where it all begins

Spin the slot with energy and out come gold, symbols, cards, and quests. Collecting specific symbols earns bonus gold, and certain combinations trigger raid and defense opportunities.

02

Treasure Card — A yield-bearing asset

Every stage features three treasure items that symbolize its world's wealth. Buy and upgrade with gold to activate, and active cards automatically produce tokens every hour.

A shared card UI with different treasure icons. A passive-yield structure.

03

NOA — The manager

A cat proxying for the god of luck, and the keeper of your treasure cards. Mysterious but perpetually bothered — a tsundere companion who reluctantly supports your growth.

Guide for the treasure card system and visual narrator of the token production and collection flow.

04

Raid & PvP

Slot outcomes can give you chances to attack other players' treasures, defend, or join raids. It's more than a slot — the social PvP layer is alive here.

05

LP (Lupin Points) — Cross-Game Asset

Every play earns LP. LP isn't a simple point — it's a composite asset.

  • • ① In-game currency — earn and spend inside Lupin Spin
  • • ② Cross-game asset — use in other DreamDo games via Lupin Hub
  • • ③ Exchangeable — swap for tickets and rewards at a specific moment
  • • ④ Growth-sharing — DreamDo's growth, shared with you
06

Telegram Native

Play directly in Telegram — no separate app install. Open @LupinSpinDevBot and tap Play to start instantly.

Lore — The great misunderstanding and the true treasure

Lupin, a genius thief who lusts after every rare thing in the world. His one life's purpose: filling his hideout with one-of-a-kind spoils.

One day, drawn by the aura of wealth emanating from the treasures, a mysterious cat named NOA appears. Perpetually bothered, NOA offers to manage the treasures — and an odd cohabitation begins.

NOA refines the treasures' aura into round tokens. Lupin dismisses them as mere "shiny scraps," but in truth they're the purest essence of wealth — more precious than any gold.

Note: Base gameplay is free and available with just a Telegram account. There's no real-money betting.
